## Accepted

### Offline Reward Perturbation Boosts Distributional Shift in Online RL
**40th Conference on Uncertainty in Artificial Intelligence (UAI), 2024**

Proposes a stealthy data poisoning attack for offline-to-online reinforcement learning that preserves offline performance while inducing harmful distribution shift during online fine-tuning.

[[abstract](/publication/2024)] [[pdf](https://openreview.net/pdf?id=wbwTF909Ve)]

### Poisoning Generative Replay in Continual Learning to Promote Forgetting
**40th International Conference on Machine Learning (ICML), 2023**

Studies poisoning attacks against generative replay in continual learning, showing how poisoned generators can promote forgetting while maintaining strong performance on the current task.

[[abstract](/publication/2023)] [[pdf](https://proceedings.mlr.press/v202/kang23c/kang23c.pdf)]

### Data-Independent Memory Hard Functions: New Attacks and Stronger Constructions
**39th Annual International Cryptology Conference (CRYPTO), 2019**

Analyzes the security of data-independent memory-hard functions, introducing new attacks and stronger constructions with improved resistance to known pebbling attacks.

[[abstract](/publication/2019)] [[pdf](https://eprint.iacr.org/2018/944.pdf)]

## Preprints / Under Review

### Adaptive Data Harvesting for Universal-Constraint Learning
**Under review, IJCAI 2026**

Formulates collocation selection as a sequential decision-making problem and learns an adaptive sampling policy that improves convergence, stability, and constraint satisfaction in universal-constraint learning settings such as Lyapunov neural networks and physics-informed neural networks.
