I am an Applied Scientist at Amazon Alexa. I received my Ph.D. in Computer Science from the University of Illinois Chicago in 2025, where I was advised by [Prof. Xinhua Zhang](https://www.cs.uic.edu/~zhangx/). My research focuses on the robustness and trustworthiness of machine learning involving temporal data, including continual learning, reinforcement learning, and language models.

Prior to UIC, I worked with [Prof. Eli Upfal](https://cs.brown.edu/people/eupfal/) and [Dr. Cyrus Cousins](https://cs.brown.edu/~ccousins/) on statistical machine learning at Brown University, where I earned my M.S. in Computer Science. I hold a B.S. in Mathematics and Computer Science from Pennsylvania State University, where I was supervised by [Prof. James Wang](https://wang.ist.psu.edu/docs/home.shtml). I also worked with [Prof. Jeremiah Blocki](https://www.cs.purdue.edu/homes/jblocki/) as a visiting research assistant at Purdue University.
